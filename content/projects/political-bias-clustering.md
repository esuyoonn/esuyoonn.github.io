*Jul 2025 – Aug 2025*

## Overview

Investigated political bias among major Korean news outlets through unsupervised clustering based on sentiment features extracted from article text.

## Approach

1. **Data Collection** — Crawled news articles from major Korean media organizations across a range of political topics
2. **Sentiment Scoring** — Scored articles at two levels:
   - *Word-level*: lexicon-based sentiment scoring per token
   - *Contextual-level*: model-based contextual sentiment analysis
3. **Clustering** — Grouped media organizations using sentiment-based feature vectors to reveal political reporting tendencies

## Results

Identified distinct clusters among Korean news outlets, with sentiment patterns aligning broadly with known political leanings — providing a data-driven lens on media bias.

## Technologies
Python, pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, web crawling
