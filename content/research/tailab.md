**tAILab (Translational AI Lab)**
Yonsei University College of Medicine
*July 2025 – Present*

Principal Investigator: Prof. Hwiyoung Kim

## Work

**VLM-based Pressure Ulcer Assessment**
Developing a synthetic data generation pipeline to address data scarcity in training a vision-language model for pressure ulcer assessment. The pipeline augments limited clinical image data with realistic synthetic samples to improve model generalizability.

**Biocompatibility Prediction for Biomaterials**
Building a prediction model to assess the biocompatibility of biomaterials, integrating molecular and structural features to support materials screening in biomedical research.

## Tools & Methods
PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers, Python, vision-language models (VLMs), synthetic data generation
